# Content Strategy

## Purpose Hierarchy

1. **Professional credibility** — establish Ahmed as a serious student at the intersection of law and technology
2. **Digital presence** — a permanent, owned platform that ranks well for his name
3. **Portfolio foundation** — structured to accept projects and writing as they develop
4. **Business card** — tutoring services with clear pricing and contact
5. **Long-term platform** — architecture supports future content (articles, projects, newsletter)

## Audience

Primary: Academics, legal professionals, technology professionals, potential employers  
Secondary: Prospective tutoring students and their parents  
Tertiary: Other students in law/computing dual-degree programs

## Tone

- Professional and intellectually serious
- Forward-looking without overstating current experience
- Specific — avoids generic law/tech buzzwords
- Personal without being informal

## Page Content Summary

### Homepage (`/`)

- **Hero**: Name, credentials, location, one-line positioning
- **About**: 3 paragraphs — academic focus, dual-degree rationale, tutoring as secondary activity
- **Interests**: 9 professional interest areas with one-line descriptions
- **Education**: ANU dual-degree with expected graduation
- **Skills**: Technical (programming, frameworks, security) and legal competencies
- **Projects preview**: Placeholder for future projects with "in development" framing
- **Contact**: Inline form for all enquiry types

### Projects (`/projects`)

Current state: coming soon placeholder with architectural scaffolding for future entries.  
Future: real project cards with slug-based detail pages.

### Tutoring (`/tutoring`)

Complete information page:
- Year 11–12: Physics, Mathematics, English, Legal Studies (ACT BSSS)
- Years 7–10: all non-science subjects
- Pricing: $50/hr online, $60/hr in person (ANU + Canberra)
- FAQ: 5 common questions
- Enquiry form pre-filled with "Tutoring enquiry" subject

### Legal Pages

- `/legal/terms`: Terms of Use with explicit AI scraping prohibition
- `/legal/privacy`: Privacy Policy compliant with Australian Privacy Act 1988

## Content Governance

- **Tone check**: no buzzwords, no marketing language, no exaggerated claims
- **Date accuracy**: graduation year (2031), pricing, availability should be kept current
- **Image**: lawyer.jpg is used as a greyscale parallax element — maintain monochrome aesthetic
